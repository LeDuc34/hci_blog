Bad one Link to heading

An example of bad affordance is the numlock button on my laptop’s keyboard.


Indeed i can’t see if the num lock is activated or not. A way to improve could be to add a little led to inform the user about the current mod.

Good one Link to heading

A good design I’ve found in my daily life is the way to authentificated to my gym. To enter in the gym, you need to show the badge and put your index on a detector to see if the fingerprint matches with the badge owner.
It seems a bit too much but infact it is quite fast : not very demanding for the users.
It solve the problem of badge sharing between users : with this design is quite diffcult to defraud.
